# ArogyaAI Overview

ArogyaAI is a gamified AI-powered healthcare assistant built to predict and prevent
lifestyle diseases (diabetes, hypertension, cardiovascular issues, obesity, mental
health disorders). Users sign in with Google, go through a conversational onboarding
chatbot that collects their health profile instead of a boring form, and land on a
dashboard that pulls real-time fitness data from their watch or phone. A guardrailed
LLM chatbot answers health queries only when backed by internet-verified sources,
recommends nearby clinics using a trust score plus GPS, and assigns daily health
tasks that users can choose from. A public leaderboard (name + points only, no
personal data) adds social motivation. Everything feeds into a disease risk engine
that continuously updates as new fitness data arrives.

## Vision
To make preventive healthcare accessible, engaging, and personalized for every
individual by turning daily health habits into a game that saves lives before a
doctor's visit becomes necessary.

## Mission
To build an AI-driven companion that understands each user's unique health context,
predicts lifestyle disease risks early, and guides them through small daily actions
that make prevention feel rewarding, not clinical.
